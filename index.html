Lisheen Nursing Home has the capacity to accommodate a maximum of 112 residents. We accommodate both female and male residents of all age groups with the following care needs:

General care, Young Chronic Sick, Mental and Physical Illness, Long Term care, Day care, Dementia care, Palliative care, Physical Disabilities and Intellectual Disabilities

Our ethos is to put the interests of the resident at the heart of our decision making and aim to provide high quality person centred nursing care to all residents of our nursing home.
