Lisheen Nursing Home is a purpose-built privately-owned nursing home run by Kevin and Geraldine Joy. Lisheen has been operated by us since October 1988. Their daughter, Valerie Joy is the Person in Charge of Care and holds an MSc in Nursing. Lisheen is a two-storey building, built on a landscaped site with views of the countryside. We are located in Rathcoole Village just off the N7 in South County Dublin. Rathcoole is approximately 10 miles from Dublin City Centre, close to shops, community centre, churches and Avoca Handweavers.

Our ethos is to put the interests of the resident at the heart of our decision making and aim to provide high quality person centred nursing care to all residents of our nursing home. We will endeavour to always comply with the Health Act 2007, the Care and Welfare Regulations 2009, The National Quality Standards For Residential Care Settings for Older People in Ireland and all other relevant legislation and standards.
